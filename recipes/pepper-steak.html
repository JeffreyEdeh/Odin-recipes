Ingredients

  • 2 pounds beef sirloin
  • 1 cube beef Bouillon
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • ¾ teaspoon garlic powder, or to taste
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • ¼ cup hot water
  • 2 large green bell peppers, roughly chopped
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can stewed tomatoes, with liquid
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Prep all Ingredients. Dice the veggies and cut the beef into strips
  2. Season the beef. Heat vegetable oil in the skillet and sear both sides of the beef strips. Transfer to a slow cooker
  3. Disslove the boullion cube and cornstarch in a bowl with hot water
  4. pour the mixture into the slow cooker then add in your vegetables, salt, sugar and soy sauce
  5. Cook on high heat for 3-4 hours or low heat for 6-8 hours
