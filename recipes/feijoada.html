Slow cooked Pepper Steak

Description

Feijoada is a Mozambican curry dish that is commonly had with rice and a salad. It's commonly mae with an assortment of meats or just pork.

Ingredients

  • Long grain rice
  • Pork belly
  • 2 cans of tomatoes
  • Lemon juice
  • Garlic
  • Lamb mix
  • Curry powder
  • 2 Red kidney beans
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Mixed Herbs
  • Chicken stock Pots
  • All purpose seasoning
  • 1 tablespoon of paprika

Steps

  1. Fry the pork and put in a bowl afterwards
  2. Fry the onions and garlic
  3. Add in your spices. Add a lot of curry
  4. Add tomatoes, beans and some lemon juice
  5. Allow to simmmer for 5 minutes then add your pork and leave to simmer for 25 minutes more minutes
